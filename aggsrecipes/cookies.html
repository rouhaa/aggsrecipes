Ingredients
  • 125 g butter
  • 1⁄2 c sugar
  • 1⁄2 c brown sugar - lightly packed
  • 1⁄2 t vanilla essence
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1⁄2 c flour
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1⁄2 c chocolate chips
Method:
  • 1. Cream butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
  • 2. Beat in vanilla essence and egg.
  • 3. Blend in sifted flour and baking powder.
  • 4. Add in chocolate chips and stir.
  • 5. Roll into small balls, place on a greased baking tray and flatten with a fork.
  • 6. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  • 7. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving.
